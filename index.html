Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2024

Welcome to Flagger. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Authentication information (email, name) when you create an account
  • Device motion data during race sessions
  • Session participation data
  • Technical information about your device and connection

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Flagger service
  • Process and analyze race performance
  • Enable multiplayer race sessions
  • Improve our service
  • Communicate with you about the service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication for user credentials
  • Firebase Realtime Database for race sessions
  • Local device storage for race history

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and real-time data
  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Apple Sign-In for authentication
  • Shake for bug reporting

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: your.email@example.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
